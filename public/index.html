About Our Service

Our URL shortener allows you to create short, easy-to-share links that redirect to your longer URLs. Whether you want to share content on social media, send links through email, or keep track of your links, our service makes it simple and efficient.

How It Works

1. Enter the long URL you want to shorten in the provided form.
2. Click the "Generate and Show" button to create your short link.
3. Copy the generated short link and share it wherever you want!
